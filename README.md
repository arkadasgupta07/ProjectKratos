## ⚡ Core Engineering Domains

This repository primarily hosts my work in the domains of **Electronics, Controls and Automation**, aiming to build robust hardware and intelligent software stacks that drive our rovers across simulated and physical extraterrestrial terrains.

### 🔌 Electronics & Hardware
Focuses on the power, communication, and physical execution of the rover.
* **Power Distribution:** Custom-designed Power Distribution Boards (PDBs).
* **Embedded Systems:** Microcontroller programming and sensor integration.
* **Circuit Design:** Hardware schematics, PCB design, and component selection.
  
### 🤖 Automation & Controls
Focuses on making the rover intelligent and fully autonomous. 
* **Navigation Stack:** ROS/ROS2, Nav2, and SLAM for terrain mapping and path planning.
* **Computer Vision:** OpenCV and ArUCO marker detection for precise localization and object search.
* **Simulation:** Ground control stations and turtlebot/rover physics simulations in Gazebo.
